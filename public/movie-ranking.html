The best animated shows according to Somebody

Somebodies top animated shows of all times

Cyberpunk Edgerunner

Good art, good story and amazing action.
Singlehandedly saved the Cyberpunk 2077 game from being a total blowout and brought lots of player into the game.

Arcane

The best animated show to come out in recent times. Riot and Fortiche did an mazing job bringing a small bit of the world of Runterra to life.
The characters, the art style, the story, the pacing and the music were amazing. Definitely a must watch for everyone.

The Fate Series

Fate has been a personal favourite show of mine for many years and with UFO Table doing the animations its even more amazing.
